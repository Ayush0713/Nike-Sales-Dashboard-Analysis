# Nike-Sales-Dashboard-Analysis (Using MS Excel and Power BI)

# Project Objective
The goal of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that help stakeholders quickly understand overall performance and identify growth opportunities.

## Dataset Used
- <a href="https://github.com/Ayush0713/Nike-Sales-Dashboard-Analysis/blob/main/Nike%20Sales%20Dashboard.pbix">Dataset </a>

## Question(KPIs)
- How does the monthly revenue trend change throughout the year, and which months perform the best or worst.
- Which product line generates the highest revenue, and which one is the most profitable.
- Who are the top 10 best-selling products, and what percentage do they contribute to total revenue.
- How is revenue distributed across regions, and which region performs the best.
- Which sales channel (Retail or Online) contributes more to revenue.
- Which gender category (Men, Women, Kids) drives the most sales.
- Dashboard Interaction <a href="https://github.com/Ayush0713/Nike-Sales-Dashboard-Analysis/commit/c390d5615143011bc3c1902a05c87d0477f6d5ff">dashboard </a>

## Process
- Cleaned and prepared raw Nike sales data in Excel by removing duplicates, handling missing values, correcting data types, and standardizing categories.
- Imported cleaned data into Power BI and built a structured data model with proper relationships and hierarchies.
- Created key DAX measures, including Total Revenue, Units Sold, Profit Margin, and Average Discount to support KPI analysis.
- Designed interactive visualizations such as monthly revenue trends, regional performance, product line comparison, and top 10 product analysis.
- Added slicers for dynamic filtering across Region, Product, Gender Category, and Sales Channel.
- Generated actionable insights on top-performing regions, profitable product lines, customer segment contributions, and seasonal revenue patterns.

## Insights
- Lifestyle and Soccer product lines generate the highest revenue.
- Kolkata region leads in total revenue performance.
- Balanced contribution from Kids, Men, and Women categories.
- Seasonal trends show strong revenue months like January, April & June.
- Clear visibility into product-level and region-wise performance.

## Final Conclusion
This project demonstrates strong skills in data cleaning, dashboard design, DAX, and data-driven storytelling. It reflects how analytical tools can turn raw data into actionable business insights.
